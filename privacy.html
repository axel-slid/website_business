Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SiteRocket, Reid, and Alex ("SiteRocket," "we," "us," or "our") collect, use, share, and protect information when you visit our website, book a call, contact us, or work with us.

We collect information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, business name, project details, website needs, scheduling information, messages, files, photos, logos, and other materials you send us.

We may also receive basic technical information when you visit the site, such as browser type, device information, approximate location derived from IP address, pages visited, and timestamps. Some of this information may be collected by third-party services used to host, schedule, secure, or operate the website.

We use information to:

  • improve our services and website.

3. Third-Party Services

These providers may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms. We do not control their systems, security practices, or data handling.

4. Payments

If you pay us through a third-party payment provider, that provider may collect payment details directly. We do not intend to store full credit card numbers on our own website.
